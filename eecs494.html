In my senior year at the University of Michigan, I've been taking a course in game development called EECS 494. Here you will find a couple of projects I have worked on in this course.

Metroid

Our first project was to recreate Metroid for the NES using Unity. My partner and I created an accurate version of a portion of the Brinstar level, and also added a custom mechanic -- the warp shot. You can find a link to the builds here.

Push

The second project I completed in the course was a game of my creation, focused on a mechanic where the player can redirect objects in the world around them. You can find a link to the builds here.

Claire and Netty

The third and largest project I completed in EECS 494 was a game called Claire and Netty, which I created with three other teammates.
