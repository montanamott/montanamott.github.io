> Project Madrigal

Project Madrigal is a research project of mine for EECS 499 at the University of Michigan, in which the goal is to create interactive raymarched geometry that integrates seamlessly with rasterized geometry in areas of rendering and physics. This page will contain links to weekly devlog posts regarding the continuous development of Project Madrigal.
